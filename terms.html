Terms & Conditions — Data Use (Demo)

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Overview

This web-based prototype is provided for research and demonstration purposes. By using this demo and registering an account you consent to the limited data collection described below.

Data We Collect

  • Account information you provide (name, email, role, password).
  • Assignments, reminders, and activity saved in your browser (stored in localStorage).
  • Basic usage interactions for local demo purposes only — no analytics are collected by default.

How We Use Data

Data is used to power the prototype features: authentication (client-side), listing and posting assignments, and scheduling reminders. Because this is a browser-only demo, data remains on your device unless you share it manually.

LocalStorage & Prototype Notice

This demo stores data in your browser's localStorage. LocalStorage is not encrypted and can be cleared by the user or by the browser. Do not use real passwords or sensitive personal data with this prototype.

Consent & Rights

By registering you consent to storing your provided data in the browser for the purposes described. You may delete your data by clearing localStorage for this site or by using the account deletion feature if available.

Contact

Questions or requests about your data: research@example.com.
